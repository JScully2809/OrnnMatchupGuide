Items

Tank items require adapting to the enemy comp as much as possible. Always build towards the biggest threat to you and your team while matching the strength to match the enemy top laner as well. Build optimally so you're strong at all times and not delaying your build because games end quickly. Try to keep yourself and your team alive until you get objectives and start winning fights


Mythics - When and Why?

Sunfire Aegis

When you are ahead of your laner and are looking for extended fights, preferably against melee

Frostfire Gauntlet

Against ranged or you're not far ahead and not looking to 1v1 your enemy laner. Just looking to be a good tank and peel

Locket of the Iron Solari

Against comps that do alot of damage to your squishies and/or if you have a hyper carry and want to protect them.

Evenshroud

If you're only looking to teamfight and against a lane where your goal is to survive until teamfights happen


Boots

Plated Steelcaps

Build when the enemy team has 3+ AD champions

Mercury Treads

Build when the enemy team has 3+ AP champions or into very heavy CC Comps

Sorcerer's Shoes

Build against very tanky comps but it's not needed if your team has enough damage

Ionian Boots of Lucidity

Build when you don't need resistances and are the only one left to carry

Boots of Swiftness

Build into specific lanes with consistent slows E.G Sion, Dr. Mundo, Illaoi, Malphite etc.


Armour

Thornmail

Build into bruisers and/or when you need healing reduction

Force of Nature

Build into mages and AP heavy champs

Anathema's Chains

Build into hard carry tops and when enemy ADC is ahead

Warmog's Armour

Build when you have enough resistances and are trying to fight and carry. Can also build into ranged top laners as a Tech

Frozen Heart

Build into fast champs with high Attack speed E.G. Tryndamere, Yasuo, Yone, etc. (Excluding Master Yi)

Winters Approach

BUILD WITH CAUTION - Okay into tank matchups - Garen and Poppy are the only acceptable matchups for Fimbulwinter

Abyssal Mask

Build when your team is AP heavy and the Enemy are stacking Magic Resistance

Gargoyle Stoneplate

Build into teams that have alot of mixed damage 2nd Item. Or build as a 4th item when you need resistances and teamfighting power
